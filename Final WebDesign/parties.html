CossiePlay Party

On the first Saturday of every month we hold a "CossiePlay Party".Party details and Entry Fee are as follows:

  • Venue: Town Hall
  • Timings: 6:00pm-11:00pm
  • Fee for Adults: $15
  • Fee for Children (6-17yrs): $10
  • Under 6: Free

The parties are a great way to be creative and socialise with other cosplay fans. Dressing up is fun! You could be apart of the world's largest cosplay community.

Each entry includes one non-alcoholic drink and a glow stick.
