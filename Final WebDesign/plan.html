Goals

A lcoally run family business which was established in 2012, with a goal to appeal towards the young audience, CossiePlay wants to expand the business by going online. With a ever increasing amount of CosPlayers from all age groups, CossiePlay aim to have a creative and attractive website primarily aimed for the younger generation, apart from the second-hand costumes.

Success Evaluation

The younger generation being the main target audience, CossiePlay website needs to be unique in its own way and have a proper plan & strategy when designing the site. The website will be built in a way which will have a clear navigation towards all the pages on the site apart from providing useful information and being attractive. Finally, being in this field of industry for over 5 years, social networking and Search Engine Optimisation with proper marketing will help reach the maximum target audience group within a short period of time.

Target Audience

Description
The concept of building CossiePlay was to reach out to all age-groups of target audience across different areas of Hulkstown, and primarily younger generations.
Primary Users
Young Generation CosPlayer's renting out for parties.
Secondary Users
Any customer looking to buy customes for other events and parties.
User Characteristics
- Age / Gender: 15+ / Male and female
- Education: Secondary school++
- Computer and web experience: Online shopping experience
